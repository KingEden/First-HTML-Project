APPLE

Apple is considered as top ranked brand worldwide.Apple has a branding strategy that focuses on the emotions. The starting point is how an Apple product experience makes you feel. The Apple brand personality is about lifestyle; imagination; liberty regained; innovation; passion; hopes, dreams and aspirations; and power-to-the-people through technology.

SAMSUNG

SAMSUNG is top ranked technology brand.Samsung was founded by Lee Byung-chul in 1938 as a trading company.Samsung entered the electronics industry in the late 1960s and the construction and shipbuilding industries in the mid-1970s. Samsung industrial affiliates include Samsung Electronics (the world's largest information technology company, consumer electronics maker and chipmaker measured by 2017 revenues), Samsung Heavy Industries (the world's 2nd largest shipbuilder measured by 2010 revenues),and Samsung Engineering and Samsung C&T (respectively the world's 13th and 36th largest construction companies).

MICROSOFT

Microsoft is high ranked American multinational technology company.It develops, manufactures, licenses, supports, and sells computer software, consumer electronics, personal computers, and related services. Its best known software products are the Microsoft Windows line of operating systems, the Microsoft Office suite, and the Internet Explorer and Edge web browsers.it was the world's largest software maker by revenue as of 2016.It is considered one of the Big Five companies in the U.S. information technology industry, along with Google, Apple, Amazon, and Facebook.

SONY

Sony operates as one of the world's largest manufacturers of consumer and professional electronic products, the largest video game console company.Sony, with its 50 percent market share in the image sensor market, is among the semiconductor sales leaders.Sony is aware to need of marketing in modern world,where comprtition is high,it takes steps towards publicizing the brands through digital marketing,advertisments to stay in limelight.

HUAWEI

Huawei designs, develops, and sells telecommunications equipment and consumer electronics.It is also top ranked technology brand. Huawei has deployed its products and services in more than 170 countries.It also has 16 research institutes in the United States, India, Sweden, Russia, China and other places. In the rapidly changing and growing field of science and technology, Huawei already has world-class innovation and R&D capabilities, and remains committed to innovation and R&D in many scientific and technological fields.
